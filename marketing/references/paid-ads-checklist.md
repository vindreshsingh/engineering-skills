# Paid Ads Checklist

Quick reference alongside [[paid-ads]].

## Pre-launch

- [ ] Budget, goal, target CAC defined
- [ ] Landing page live with UTM support
- [ ] Conversion pixel/event tested (fires on sign-up)
- [ ] One platform selected for initial test

## Creative

- [ ] 3+ ad variants (pain, proof, demo)
- [ ] Product screenshot or demo in every ad
- [ ] Landing headline matches ad hook
- [ ] No false claims

## Weekly optimization

- [ ] CTR, CPC, conversion rate, CAC reviewed
- [ ] Losers paused (low CTR or CAC > 2× target)
- [ ] Winners scaled ≤20% budget increase
- [ ] Results reported to growth-lead

## Compliance

- [ ] Privacy policy on landing page
- [ ] Platform ad policies reviewed
