# Profile: solo-founder

For one person taking an idea to a launched, growing product. Always-on: `agent-guardrails`. Route via
`skill-router`; these are the skills to **prioritize** — lean, ship-oriented, skip the heavy process.

- **Define:** product-discovery, idea-shaping, spec-first (keep it light)
- **Build:** incremental-delivery, test-first, ui-craft, react-patterns, llm-feature-engineering,
  context-curation, simplify
- **Verify:** browser-checks, experimentation, fault-recovery
- **Review:** review-gate, hardening (just the essentials)
- **Ship:** git-flow, feature-flags, launch-readiness
- **Grow:** launch-campaign, growth-strategy, seo-growth, content-marketing, social-distribution,
  referral-loop
- **Autonomy (force-multiplier for a solo dev):** autonomous-loops, agent-verification, parallel-subagents

De-emphasize unless it bites: migration-path, observability at scale, finops-budget, formal grooming.
Bias to shipping and measuring over ceremony.
